About Me

My name is Xuefeng Wang,I am a Chinese Canadian, and I am a full time student in the Interactive Media Design Program at Algonquin College.

In the past two years of study and practice of Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College, I have mastered a number of graphic design skills such as InDesign、Illustrator、XD and I have in-depth knowledge of AfterEffect. I can skillfully use WordPress to independently design and run a company's website. I am proficient in using social media such as Facebook,Instagram, Twitter, TikTok etc.

I have good communication skills, I have a strong desire and ability to continue learning new things, and I am confident that I will be able to perform the duties of a web designer
